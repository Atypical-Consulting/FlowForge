## Notes

Animation components are ready for use throughout the app. They include:
- `AnimatedList` / `AnimatedListItem` - Staggered entrance animations for lists
- `FadeIn` - Simple fade-in with variants (default, up, scale)
- Reusable variants: `staggerContainer`, `staggerItem`, `fadeIn`, `fadeInUp`, `fadeInScale`, `slideInLeft`, `tabContent`

The `staggerLimit` parameter (default 15) prevents performance issues with long lists.
